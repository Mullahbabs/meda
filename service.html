What We Stand On

Our Objectives

Promoting Change

Promoting a change in perception and attitude is vital to fostering an entrepreneurial spirit in Cross River State. MEDA aims to reorient individuals towards private enterprise, encouraging innovation and creativity while reducing reliance on paid employment. By inspiring the next generation of entrepreneurs, MEDA drives a deliberate shift towards self-sustainability and economic empowerment. This change not only uplifts individuals but also strengthens the foundation of the stateâ€™s economy.

Our Objectives

Infrastructure Development

Strengthening infrastructure is essential for sustainable MSME growth. MEDA is committed to building technical and managerial capacities through strategic partnerships and collaborations. By fostering an environment equipped with robust facilities and resources, the Agency supports entrepreneurs in achieving operational excellence. Infrastructural development paves the way for improved efficiency, innovation, and the competitive advantage needed for businesses to thrive in a dynamic marketplace.

Our Objectives

Access To Finance

Access to finance is a critical enabler for business success. MEDA facilitates this by collaborating with financial institutions to create accessible and affordable funding schemes. These initiatives are tailored to meet the unique needs of MSMEs, ensuring that entrepreneurs have the resources to innovate, expand, and sustain their operations. By addressing financial barriers, MEDA fosters economic inclusion and drives the growth of local enterprises.

Our Objectives

Facilitating Linkages

Linkages with business associations such as MAN, NASSI, NASME, and CALCCIMA are key to unlocking growth opportunities. MEDA bridges the gap between local entrepreneurs and established networks, providing access to mentorship, resources, and markets. These collaborations empower businesses to gain exposure, leverage expertise, and harness the collective strength of industry associations to achieve long-term success.
