Program Overview

The REDI programme is a proactive and compassionate response designed to provide a structured and supportive pathway for civil and public servants to transition to retirement with dignity and confidence.

This comprehensive strategy empowers retirees to build fulfilling and financially secure lives beyond their formal careers through entrepreneurship development, financial planning, and community support systems.

Program Objectives

Pre-Retirement Planning

Comprehensive education and planning for impending retirees to prepare them for life after public service.

Entrepreneurial Skills

Practical training in business management, idea development, and enterprise creation tailored for retirees.

Financial Literacy

Specialized financial management training for retirement income, savings, and investment strategies.

Post-Retirement Support

Ongoing mentorship and advisory services to help retirees navigate their new entrepreneurial journey.

Community Engagement

Programs to maintain social connections and integrate retirees into productive community roles.

Health & Well-Being

Holistic support programs addressing physical and mental health in retirement.

Program Details

Program Structure

The REDI program operates through three key phases:

  1. Transition Phase (Pre-Retirement): 6-month preparatory program for soon-to-retire civil servants
  2. Launch Phase (0-12 months post-retirement): Intensive entrepreneurship training and business setup support
  3. Sustain Phase (Ongoing): Continuous mentorship, networking, and resource access

Eligibility Criteria

To participate in REDI, applicants must meet these requirements:

  • Current or former Cross River State civil/public servant
  • Within 2 years pre-retirement or up to 5 years post-retirement
  • Demonstrated interest in entrepreneurship
  • Commitment to participate in program activities
  • Willingness to mentor other retirees

Program Benefits

REDI participants receive:

  • Comprehensive pre-retirement planning workshops
  • Specialized entrepreneurship training for retirees
  • Access to retirement business grants
  • One-on-one business counseling
  • Health and wellness programs
  • Alumni network and peer support groups
  • Market access and business linkage opportunities

Focus Areas

REDI focuses on business sectors well-suited for retirees:

Consulting Services

  • Professional consulting
  • Training services
  • Public sector expertise

Agribusiness

  • Small-scale farming
  • Agro-processing
  • Agricultural consulting

Community Services

  • Adult education
  • Childcare services
  • Community leadership

Heritage & Culture

  • Cultural preservation
  • Tourism services
  • Traditional crafts
