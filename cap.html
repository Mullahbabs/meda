Program Overview

Cross River State, a region brimming with artistic talent and cultural vibrancy, is poised to become a thriving hub for the creative economy. However, many talented creatives face significant challenges in skills development, mentorship, and market access.

The Creative Accelerator Programme (CAP) has been strategically designed to address these critical needs and unlock the immense potential of the state's creative sector through comprehensive support and development initiatives.

Program Objectives

Skills Development

Providing essential technical and business skills training for creative professionals.

Market Access

Creating pathways to local and international markets for creative works.

Targeted Mentorship

Connecting emerging creatives with established industry professionals.

Creative Ecosystem

Building collaborative networks and support systems for creatives.

Funding Opportunities

Facilitating access to grants, investments, and financial resources.

Digital Integration

Equipping creatives with digital tools and online presence strategies.

Program Details

Program Structure

CAP operates through three intensive phases:

  1. Discovery Phase (2 months): Skills assessment and creative business fundamentals
  2. Development Phase (4 months): Specialized training and project incubation
  3. Showcase Phase (2 months): Market readiness preparation and exhibition opportunities

Ongoing mentorship continues for 6 months post-program.

Eligibility Criteria

To participate in CAP, creatives must:

  • Be residents of Cross River State
  • Be aged 18-45 years
  • Demonstrate creative talent or business potential
  • Have at least 2 years of creative practice (for advanced tracks)
  • Commit to full program participation
  • Show willingness to collaborate with other creatives

Program Benefits

CAP participants receive:

  • World-class creative industry training
  • One-on-one mentorship from industry leaders
  • Access to creative workspaces and equipment
  • Opportunities to showcase work at exhibitions
  • Business development support
  • Networking with investors and buyers
  • Seed funding for outstanding projects

Creative Disciplines

CAP focuses on these creative sectors:

Visual Arts

  • Painting & sculpture
  • Photography
  • Digital art

Performing Arts

  • Music
  • Dance
  • Theater

Design & Craft

  • Fashion design
  • Jewelry making
  • Textile arts

Media & Content

  • Film production
  • Creative writing
  • Animation
