Program Overview

The Agricultural Development and Empowerment Programme (ADEP) is a transformative three-year initiative strategically designed to unlock the untapped potential of youth and women in Cross River State through structured and sustainable agribusiness activities.

Spanning from 2024 to 2027, ADEP is more than just a program; it's a comprehensive strategy to build resilient communities, drive economic diversification, and foster a culture of entrepreneurship within the agricultural sector.

Program Objectives

Enhancing Livelihood

Improving income and quality of life for youth and women through viable agribusiness opportunities.

Sustainable Practices

Promoting climate-smart and environmentally friendly agricultural techniques.

Value Chain Development

Strengthening all aspects of agricultural production, processing and distribution.

Capacity Building

Comprehensive training in modern agricultural techniques and business management.

Access to Finance

Facilitating microloans, grants and resources for agricultural startups.

Market Linkages

Connecting producers with buyers and adding value to agricultural products.

Gender & Youth Focus

Special emphasis on empowering women and young people in agriculture.

Environmental Care

Promoting practices that protect ecosystems while boosting productivity.

Program Details

Program Structure

ADEP operates through a three-year phased approach:

  1. Year 1: Community mobilization, baseline surveys, and foundational training
  2. Year 2: Intensive skills development, cooperative formation, and pilot projects
  3. Year 3: Business scaling, market linkages, and sustainability planning

Continuous monitoring and evaluation throughout all phases.

Eligibility Criteria

To participate in ADEP, applicants must:

  • Be residents of Cross River State
  • Be between 18-45 years old (for youth category)
  • Demonstrate interest in agriculture/agribusiness
  • Commit to full program participation
  • Have access to land (owned or leased) for farming activities
  • Be willing to join or form agricultural cooperatives

Program Benefits

ADEP participants receive:

  • Comprehensive agribusiness training and certification
  • Access to improved seedlings and farming inputs
  • Microcredit and startup funding opportunities
  • Technical assistance and extension services
  • Market access and value addition support
  • Cooperative development and group farming support
  • Modern farming equipment access

Focus Value Chains

ADEP focuses on these key agricultural value chains:

Crop Production

  • Cassava
  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Cocoa

Livestock

  • Poultry
  • Fish farming
  • Snail rearing
  • Goat/sheep production

Agro-Processing

  • Garri production
  • Rice milling
  • Oil palm processing

Emerging Sectors

  • Beekeeping
  • Mushroom farming
  • Hydroponics
