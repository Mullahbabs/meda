15 Years Since Inception

The Microfinance & Enterprise Development Agency (MEDA) is a transformative organization dedicated to fostering economic growth, reducing poverty, and empowering entrepreneurs in Cross River State and beyond. Established to address the challenges faced by small businesses and underserved communities, MEDA provides innovative financial solutions, capacity-building programs, and strategic support to enable sustainable development..

Through its tailored microfinance initiatives, MEDA bridges the gap between financial institutions and grassroots entrepreneurs, creating opportunities for business growth, job creation, and improved livelihoods. With a strong commitment to inclusivity, the agency ensures that women, youths, and rural communities are at the forefront of its development agenda.

At MEDA, we believe that entrepreneurship is the cornerstone of economic empowerment. By providing access to funding, mentorship, and training, we help individuals unlock their potential and contribute meaningfully to the local and global economy.

Our Objectives

Objectives

Promoting Change

Promoting a change in the perception and general attitude of Cross Riverians, re-orienting them towards private enterprise by encouraging the entrepreneurial spirit and a deliberate movement away from looking for paid employment.


Infrastructure Growth

Infrastructure for MSME, building technical & managerial capacity for domestic MSMEs through strategic collaborations and partnerships with stakeholders, ensuring long-term growth and sustainability of small businesses.

Access To Finance

Enhancing MSME access to finance through various schemes undertaken by the government and selected financial institutions. These programs aim to bridge the funding gap and empower entrepreneurs across various sectors.

Facilitating Linkages

Facilitating linkages with key business associations such as MAN, NASSI, NASME, and CALCCIMA to maximize the opportunities and benefits that come with such partnerships, fostering economic growth and development within the state.

Our Programs

Our Partner Funded Programs

BOI FUND

All Access


The Bank of Industry fund is a collaborative matching fund between the Cross River State Government and the Bank of Industry on a 50/50 basis. The fund aligns with MEDA’s goals of facilitating the establishment and growth of MSMEs in the state. The total fund amount is N500,000,000.00. This fund is not limited to any particular sector; rather, it is accessible to any indigenous entrepreneur looking to grow their business.

Read More

BANK OF AGRICULTURE

Farmers And Agric Bodies


The Bank of Agriculture fund is a microcredit initiative established by the Agency in collaboration with the Bank of Agriculture on a 50/50 basis. It supports microenterprises across Cross River State. The global fund amount is N200,000,000.00. This initiative focuses on supporting microenterprises in selected sectors, providing much-needed financial assistance to drive economic activities and foster growth in these areas.

Read More

EDC SUPPORT FUND

Accessible To All


The EDC Support Fund is a funding window created by the Agency to assist graduates from the South-South Entrepreneurship Development Centre (SSEDC). This initiative is a 100-million-naira revolving MSME credit loan fund with an interest rate of 9% per annum. The fund is categorized into three parts, providing tailored support to ensure that graduates of SSEDC have the financial backing needed to start or expand their ventures.

Read More

MEDA MICRO-CREDIT

Accessible To All


The MEDA Micro Credit Scheme is designed to support youth-owned microbusinesses in Cross River State. This initiative is a collaboration between MEDA and the Ministry of Youth and Sports Development. It provides financial assistance to empower young entrepreneurs, encouraging business growth and sustainability. The scheme addresses the funding gap for youth enterprises, contributing to economic development and job creation within the state.
