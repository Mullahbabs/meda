About POISED

The People First Initiatives for Skills and Enterprise Development (POISED Project) is a comprehensive program fostering widespread economic empowerment in Cross River State. Strategically integrating multiple initiatives, POISED represents a significant stride towards a more vibrant and economically empowered Cross River.

This initiative directly reflects the 'People-First' agenda of His Excellency, Sen. Prince Bassey Edet Otu, the Executive Governor of Cross River State, by addressing the needs of diverse demographic groups through targeted programs.
