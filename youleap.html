Program Overview

The Youth Local Entrepreneurship and Acceleration Programme (YOU-LEAP) is a transformative initiative designed to empower the youth of Cross River State, both in urban centers and rural communities, to become self-reliant entrepreneurs and job creators.

This comprehensive program addresses the unique challenges faced by young entrepreneurs through skills development, access to resources, mentorship, and targeted support systems. YOU-LEAP is more than a training programâ€”it's an ecosystem for youth economic empowerment.

Program Objectives

Comprehensive Skills Development

Provide hands-on training in business management, financial literacy, digital skills, and sector-specific technical skills to prepare youth for entrepreneurial success.

Access To Resources

Facilitate access to startup funding, business tools, market linkages, and ongoing support services to help young entrepreneurs launch and grow their businesses.

Foster Innovation

Cultivate a culture of innovation, creativity, and problem-solving among youth entrepreneurs to address local challenges with sustainable business solutions.

Rural Youth Empowerment

Special focus on rural youth with tailored programs that address unique challenges and opportunities in rural economic ecosystems.

Tech Advancement

Equip urban youth with cutting-edge digital and technological skills to compete in the modern digital economy.

Well-Being Support

Provide mental health support, stress management, and personal development programs to ensure holistic entrepreneur development.

Program Details

Program Structure

The YOU-LEAP program is structured into three main phases:

  1. Foundation Phase (3 months): Intensive training in entrepreneurship fundamentals, personal development, and sector-specific skills.
  2. Incubation Phase (6 months): Hands-on business development with mentorship, prototype testing, and market validation.
  3. Acceleration Phase (3 months): Business scaling support, access to funding, and market linkages.

Participants receive continuous mentorship throughout the 12-month program and beyond.

Eligibility Criteria

To participate in YOU-LEAP, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Resident of Cross River State (urban or rural)
  • Aged between 18-45 years
  • Demonstrated interest in entrepreneurship (new or existing business)
  • Commitment to complete the full program duration
  • Willingness to participate in all program activities

Special consideration is given to women, persons with disabilities, and rural community members.

Program Benefits

Participants in the YOU-LEAP program gain access to:

  • Comprehensive entrepreneurship training and certification
  • One-on-one business mentorship from industry experts
  • Access to startup grants and low-interest loans
  • Co-working spaces and business development resources
  • Market linkages and buyer-seller meetups
  • Technology tools and digital platforms for business
  • Ongoing support through alumni networks

Target Sectors

YOU-LEAP focuses on key economic sectors with high potential for youth employment and entrepreneurship:

Agriculture & Food Processing

  • Smart farming technologies
  • Agro-processing
  • Agricultural value chains

Creative Industries

  • Fashion and design
  • Media and entertainment
  • Arts and crafts

Technology & Innovation

  • Software development
  • Digital marketing
  • Tech-enabled services

Green Economy

  • Renewable energy
  • Waste management
  • Sustainable products
